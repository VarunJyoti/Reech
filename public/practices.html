Reech App is easy to use, here are some suggestions to optimize its benefits...

Best Practices

  • Reech app can detect members of your team and automatically makes a group called "My Team”. All the members are notified about the group. Make sure to remind your team to download and activate their profile
  • Use the "My Team” to send all important announcements to everyone in your office
  • If you are part of large brokerage firm that has multiple offices and brokers, you may want to create an additional group and add all members of your office to that group
  • You can always create more groups for any other purpose such as sub-teams, all-brokers of large brokerage firm, early viewing groups are just few examples
  • You can always chat one-on-one
