Our Story
Our founder Nagendra Shukla is a licensed Realtor in CA. Reech app came in to existence out of his 12+ years of experience in the industry. Reech App intends to solve following everyday challenges for Agents and Brokers.
Agent challenges
Whether it is finding viewing information while on-the-go or socializing their coming soon listings with fellow agents without incrementing DOM or CDOM or just daily touch base with agents in contract, Agents have to resort to traditional ways of communication i.e. email or phone call. Other industries have moved on to messaging which is much more efficient and quick compared to email and calling.
Broker’s challenges
Timely and relevant communication is key for creating and maintaining top performing teams. Brokers need a tool where they can disseminate information quick and easy way instead of needing to compose an email and sending.
